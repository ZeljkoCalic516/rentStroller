About us

Rent Stroller is a small, family-run rental service based in Belgrade. We help parents and visitors travel lighter by offering clean, well-maintained baby gear for short-term use.

Our catalog includes strollers for city walks and travel, high chairs for mealtimes at home or away, and baby car seats that meet safety standards—so you can focus on your trip instead of packing everything from home.

Whether you are in town for a few days, hosting relatives, or need a spare seat or chair for a little while, we aim to make pickup and handoff simple and friendly. Browse what we have on the Rent page, or call us to check availability and arrange a time that suits you.
